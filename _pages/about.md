🖐️ Hi! I'm a Ph.D. candidate in Data Science, Analytics and Engineering at **Arizona State University**, advised by [Prof. Yalin Wang](https://gsl.lab.asu.edu/) and co-advised by [Prof. Zhong-Lin Lu](https://bcs.shanghai.nyu.edu/en/peoples/faculty-affiliates/zhong-lin-lu) (NYU). My research focuses on **geometric deep learning, medical imaging, fMRI signal analysis, brain surface mesh, computational neuroscience and generative models**.

Before this, I received my M.S. from UC San Diego and B.S. in Physics from Fudan University.

I am also working on designing **homebrew TRPG content, Tarvern-based mini RPGs, and my one-man-army indie RPG using Godot and various AI tools**.
